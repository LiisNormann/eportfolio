Häkaton 2021

Seminar information

  • Category: Seminar
  • Seminar date: October, 2021
  • Organizer: Tartu Vocational College

Häkaton was a seminar held in VOCO on the topics revolving economy and Information Technology. Several different quest lectors came to present their experience and knowledge on these topics. The students participating in the seminar were from different VOCO courses, each connected to the mentioned fields in one way or another.

During this seminar the participants were split into small teams with whom their task was to come up with an idea for a new software. The students were given no limitations on what type of software has to be brainstormed for. While presenting their ideas, the students had to follow certain guidelines given to them by the lectors.
